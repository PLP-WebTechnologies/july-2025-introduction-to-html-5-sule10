About the Workshop

The Climate Action Workshop 2025 brings together students, professionals, and communities to learn practical steps for fighting climate change. This year’s focus is on:

  • Tree planting and sustainable forestry
  • Reducing carbon footprints
  • Urban waste management and recycling
  • Climate change advocacy strategies
